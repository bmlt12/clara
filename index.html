You left earlier and I thought maybe I’d never get the chance to talk to you the way I wanted. But fate had other plans — it brought us together again at Batama Street...

Sister Adwoa, there’s something I’ve been hiding from you. I dated before, way back in 2022. It lasted for about two years, but unfortunately, she broke up with me...

But thank God you came along and changed everything. You made me forget her and gave me a reason to smile again. That’s why I got you the first necklace...

And now, Clara, I would like to get you a new necklace — something that will still show how much you mean to me as a friend I love...

I know I’ve failed you sometimes and I regret those moments. You never complained, but I know my faults. Not because I wanted to hurt you...

I remember telling girls who had crushes on me or those who thought I loved them, that I already had a beautiful, cute girlfriend...

And Clara, every time I see you rocking your dresses, I can’t help but smile. You are indeed my type — the type of woman I want to spend my days with...

Sometimes, some of your actions hurt me too much. But I think it’s normal, because you’ll never truly get hurt if you don’t love or care. And although sometimes I complain...

I know you’re with someone now and I respect that. But I can’t hide how I feel sometimes. I wish I could be the one to hold your hands...

Even if life has taken us on different paths, I’ll never stop cherishing the moments we shared. You’ll always be the one who makes my heart race...

Forever yours in thought and in feeling… ❤️
