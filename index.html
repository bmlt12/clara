My Darling Adwoa Clara…

I don’t even know how to start this the perfect way. I don’t know the perfect respectful words to use for someone like you. But I remember the very first day I saw you — at the faculty office, submitting your documents in that yellow dress...

You left earlier, and I thought maybe I’d never get the chance to talk to you the way I wanted. But fate had other plans — it brought us together again at Batama Street...

Adwoa Clara, there’s something I’ve been hiding from you. I dated before, way back in 2022. It lasted for about a year and a half, but unfortunately, she broke up with me...

But thank God you came along and changed everything. You made me forget her, made me block her, and gave me a reason to smile again...

And now, Adwoa Clara, I would like to get you a new necklace — something that will still show how much you mean to me as a friend I love...

I know I’ve failed you sometimes, and I regret those moments. You never complained, but I know my faults...

I remember telling girls who had crushes on me, or those who thought I loved them, that I already had a beautiful, cute girlfriend...

And Adwoa Clara, every time I see you rocking your dresses, I can’t help but smile. You are indeed my type...

Sometimes, some of your actions — and even the actions of others — hurt me too much. But I think it’s normal...

I know you’re with someone now, and I respect that. But I can’t hide how I feel. I wish I could be the one to hold your hands...

Even if life has taken us on different paths, I’ll never stop cherishing the moments we shared...

Forever yours in thought and in feeling… ❤️
